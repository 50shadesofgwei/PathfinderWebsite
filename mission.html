Pathfinder is a UK-based research and development company that designs and manufactures high-performance quantum information processors.

We believe there are too few serious attempts at designing quantum computers end-to-end, the way that Jobs and Wozniak did for personal computers in the late 70s.

We draw on two unique Continental advantages: the research and talent of the world's leading universities, and the expertise in precision manufacturing from the semiconductor, aerospace, and scientific instrumentation industries.

We believe that the discovery of breakthrough technology is the key driving force of civilisation.
