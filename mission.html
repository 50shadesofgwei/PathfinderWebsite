Pathfinder is a UK-based research and development company that designs and manufactures high-performance quantum information processors.

We believe there are too few serious attempts at designing quantum computers end-to-end, the way that Jobs and Wozniak did for personal computers in the late 70s.

We start at a moment when artificial intelligence makes end-to-end system design tractable for a small team, and when a large proportion of leading talent across quantum physics and semiconductor manufacturing is located throughout the UK and Europe.

We believe that the discovery of breakthrough technology is the key driving force of civilisation. Pathfinder is the way we make that discovery real: by designing quantum computers as complete systems we tackle the problem head on, with tenacity and with vision.
